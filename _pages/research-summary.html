Prof. Tong-Yee (Tony) Lee is a Chair Professor in the Computer Science and Information Engineering Department of National Cheng-Kung University, and the head of the Computer Graphics Group/Visual System Laboratory. His research interests are in multimedia retargeting, computer graphics, visualization, deep learning and medical applications. His email: tonylee@mail.ncku.edu.tw and the research summary of his major publications is listed below. Up to now, he has published (or accepted) near 90 IEEE/ACM Transactions/SIGGRAPH/SIGGRAPH Asia/CVPR papers including 13 ACM TOG and 48 IEEE TVCG papers. Both TOG and TVCG are TOP journals in computer graphics. Our 13 ACM TOG papers are also SIGGRAPH/SIGGRAPH Asia papers (total 12 siggraph/siggraph asia papers). My publication list
